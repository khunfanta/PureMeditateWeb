Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

The Short Version: Pure Meditate was built to be a sanctuary. I believe your privacy is a prerequisite for peace of mind. The app collects no personal data.

No Personal Data: I do not collect names, emails, phone numbers or any other identifying information. You don't need an account to find your peace.

No Tracking & No Ads: I do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. I have no idea who you are or how you use the app, and I never will. This is a 100% ad-free zone.

Apple Health Integration: Pure Meditate gives you the option to sync your session data with the Apple Health app (Mindful Minutes). This data is written directly to your local Apple Health database. I cannot see, access, or transmit this data. It never leaves your device and is never shared with third parties.

Local Storage Only: Your soundscape preferences and timer settings are stored locally on your device. This ensures your experience is consistent each time you open the app.

Third Parties: I do not share data with third parties because I have no data to share. I do not use any cloud servers or external databases.

Contact: If you have questions, contact me via the contact form or on Bluesky.
